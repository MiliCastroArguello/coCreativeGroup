About Us

We have more than 15 years of experience in different areas of HR. More than 10 years working together forging a great friendship. We share the passion for our profession and the motivation to help others. Co-Creating Group was born as a hobby and little by little we professionalized it and captured our knowledge; planning and putting together the strategy to help people and companies find the job that fits not only their profile but also what makes them happy.

Our Mission

Help you find the talent your organization is looking for!

Our Vision

We are united by passion, commitment and dedication. We have many years of experience and a high quality service. We want to CO-CREATE, work as a team to become YOUR strategic partner. We will think of the best action plan for YOUR search. We will design together the stages, processes and steps to follow. We are interested in sharing the challenges, estimated times, reality of each target labor market, among others. We like to be transparent and to be able to fulfill our COMMITMENT with your organization.

Our Values

We want to CO-CREATE, work as a team to become YOUR strategic partner. We will think of the best action plan for YOUR search. We will design together the stages, processes and steps to follow. We need to be CLOSE, to know each other in depth, the organization, the collaborators, their values and expectations in order to find the person who can join the team that needs him/her. Each search has its particularity. We are interested in sharing the challenges, estimated times, reality of each target labor market, among others. We like to be transparent and to be able to fulfill our COMMITMENT with your organization.
